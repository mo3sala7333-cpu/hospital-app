Version: 1.0

# Vision

Al Hayat Investor Experience is not a traditional website.

It is a cinematic, interactive digital experience designed for large-screen presentation during investor conferences.

The goal is to make every visitor feel that they are entering the future of healthcare.

The experience must communicate:

- Trust
- Innovation
- Growth
- Luxury
- Technology
- Vision

Scrolling controls a cinematic drone that tells the story of Al Hayat Medical Services.

The experience must feel closer to an Unreal Engine presentation than a conventional website.

Every movement must have purpose.

Every animation must tell part of the company's story.

The supplied real hospital buildings are the heroes of the experience.

No AI-generated hospitals.

No placeholder content.

No traditional pages.

No PowerPoint-style slides.

This project is designed to leave a lasting impression on investors.
